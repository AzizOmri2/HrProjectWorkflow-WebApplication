👤 HR PROJECT WORKFLOW

Is a platform that provides a complete and interactive space where candidates can effectively manage their job search and track every stage of the recruitment process.

  • 📝 Complete Candidate Profile:
    Each candidate creates a personalized account with essential information (name, contact details, education, experience).
    This profile is generated by AI when you upload your resume/CV.
  • 💼 Explore and Apply to Job Offers:
    All published job opportunities are displayed with full details (title, department, skills required, experience level).
    Candidates can apply to offers directly with one click.
  • 📊 Track Application Progress:
    Candidates can follow the exact status of each submitted application:
    Received → Pending → Interview Scheduled → Offer Sent / Rejected.
  • 📆 Interview Management:
    When an interview is scheduled, the platform notifies the candidate immediately. Interview details (date, time, location or meeting link) appear in the candidate's dashboard. If changes occur (rescheduling or cancellation), notifications are sent to inform the candidate.
  • ❌ Withdraw an Application:
    Before the final decision, candidates have the right to withdraw their application, especially if they are no longer interested in the position.
  • 🔔 Notifications:
    Candidates receive alerts when:
    • A new job offer is published.
    • An interview is scheduled or rescheduled.
    • The status of an application changes (Accept or Rejection).
  • 💬 IT Articles and Community Interaction:
    Beyond job offers, candidates can explore a section filled with articles on tech trends, career advice, and tools. They can also react or comment, fostering a more engaging experience.

With this module, our recruitment platform ensures that every candidate stays informed, engaged, and empowered throughout their job search journey — all in one intuitive and secure environment.
