🆘 Help & Support

Find answers to common questions and get assistance for any issues you may encounter while using our recruitment platform.

  • ❓ How do I apply for a job?
    Go to the jobs section, browse available opportunities, and click “Show Details” for any offer that suits your profile then click “Apply Now”.
  • 🔍 Where can I view my applications?
    Log in to your account and navigate to the “My Applications” section to check the progress of your submissions.
  • 📅 Where can I check my planned interviews?
    Log in to your account and navigate to the “My Interviews” section to check the interviews scheduling.
  • 🔄 Can I update my profile or resume?
    Yes. Go to your dashboard, click on “Edit Profile,” and upload a new CV or update your personal and professional information.
  • 🔐 I forgot my password. What should I do?
    On the login page, click “Forgot Password” and follow the instructions to reset your credentials via email.
